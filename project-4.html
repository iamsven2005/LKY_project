Modern House

Client
Caleigh Jerde
Location
Cincinnati, United States
Years
2019 - 2020

When it comes to designing a high-rise building or modern house, there are several design choices that need to be carefully considered. Our interior design company prioritizes a sleek and minimalist aesthetic, with clean lines and an emphasis on functionality.

For this particular high-rise building, we aimed to create an environment that feels both inviting and awe-inspiring. We chose neutral colors, such as black, white, and gray, to create a modern and sophisticated look. The use of glass and reflective surfaces allowed for natural light to flood the space, giving it an open and spacious feel. We incorporated art pieces and sculptures to add visual interest and to enhance the atmosphere of the space.

In terms of furniture and fixtures, we focused on choosing pieces that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also practical and comfortable. We opted for minimalist furniture pieces with clean lines and a simple design. The lighting was carefully chosen to enhance the atmosphere of the space and to highlight key features of the interior design.

Overall, the design choices for this high-rise building were made with both form and function in mind. We aimed to create a space that is both visually appealing and practical, with an emphasis on modernity and sophistication.
