Mediterranean Villa

Client
Caleigh Jerde
Location
Cincinnati, United States
Years
2019 - 2020

The Mediterranean region has long been a source of inspiration for interior designers, and this villa is no exception. The design team made bold choices to capture the essence of the region while maintaining a modern aesthetic. The color palette was carefully selected to complement the architecture and natural surroundings, with warm earthy tones, bright accents, and pops of blue reminiscent of the sea.

The use of textures was another defining element of the design, with natural stone and wood surfaces used throughout the villa. Arched doorways and windows, decorative tiles, and wrought iron details added to the Mediterranean charm, while modern furnishings and sleek lighting fixtures kept the space feeling current and fresh.

The villa's layout was also carefully considered to create a flow that maximized the beautiful views and natural light. Large windows and doors opened up to the outdoor spaces, blurring the lines between indoors and out. The result is a luxurious yet inviting space that perfectly captures the Mediterranean lifestyle. Overall, the interior design choices were a beautiful balance of tradition and modernity, creating a space that feels both timeless and of-the-moment.
