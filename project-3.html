Wooden House

Client
Caleigh Jerde
Location
Cincinnati, United States
Years
2019 - 2020

The interior design company aimed to create a warm and cozy atmosphere in this red wooden house near the sea. The natural surroundings served as an inspiration for the design choices. The color palette was carefully selected to complement the natural colors of the environment, including shades of blue, green, and brown.

To further enhance the coastal feel, the interior designers used textures such as wood and rope, and incorporated nautical elements like a ship wheel and anchor motifs. The use of natural wood elements was also incorporated in the flooring, walls, and ceilings, adding warmth and character to the space.

In addition, the furniture and decor were chosen to create a relaxed and inviting ambiance. Soft fabrics and comfortable seating arrangements were used throughout the space to promote relaxation and comfort. The overall design reflects a balance of modern and traditional elements that blend seamlessly to create a unique coastal aesthetic. The interior design company's careful selection of materials and attention to detail have created a harmonious and welcoming environment that complements the beauty of its natural surroundings.
